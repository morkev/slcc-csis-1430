The Semester in a Nutshell

From the time I was a small child, I have always had a humongous interest for video games, computers, and pretty much every single thing related to CPUs. Prior to taking this programming class I knew relatively little about the actual structure, and semantic that helps to create websites, animations, and almost everything.


I have appreciated the level of detail this class has provided into the science that governs our future. It has differentiated what we as humans know, from what we are still learning about, to what we do not yet understand, and the barriers we will always have. Since this semester began, I have found myself actively engaging in side-projects, and now I regularly watch videos, or read about a specific property; among other new learning opportunities. Before taking this class I was not able to follow a conversation about code, or anything related because I did not understand many of the terms being used, but now I am more fluent in the language and topics being discussed.


I have been able to draw many connections to another class I am taking this semester... I truly appreciate this class.
