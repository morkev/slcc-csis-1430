PERSEVERANCE

Perseverance is a truly remarkable vehicle, and just as Metallica, it rocks... somehow... It will be carrying numerous experiments to Mars that will not only advance the goal of sending humans to the Red Planet, but will also search for evidence that Mars may once have sustained life. Additionally, Perseverance will carry a small helicopter called Ingenuity, marking the first time that an aircraft has ever flown on any planetary body other than Earth. Not only will Ingenuity provide remarkable images of the Martian surface, but, if successful, this technology could be a valuable tool for human explorers as they utilize drones to investigate hard-to-reach locations (cliff sides, caves, etc.) and provide the ability to do advanced reconnaissance.

Despite the challenges that face us here on Earth, we are entering what will almost certainly be one of the most exciting and relevant periods in the history of space exploration. Let us regard Perseverance as a symbol of the great things we can achieve today, and in the future, when people of all backgrounds, ethnicities and nationalities work together to explore the unknown.
